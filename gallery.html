What are Uncommon Approaches?

This website's purpose is to provide simple information to those looking to learn film photography. Since you've now learned all the basic functions, materials, and processes, now it's time to impliment them and start taking your own photos!

The Gallery of Uncommon Approaches serves as a source of insapiritation and information on different techniques to take your photos beyond the basics.
